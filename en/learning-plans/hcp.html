👤 Who is this plan for?
📊 Competency level Richardson et al. 2026
Level 1 — Awareness
Non-specialist HCP
Encounters diabetes technology occasionally. Needs basic awareness, safety-critical knowledge and ability to signpost.
Level 2 — Competence
Regular diabetes care
Provides regular diabetes care. Needs to interpret data, manage common issues, understand different systems.
Level 3 — Expertise
Diabetes technology specialist
Primary role is diabetes technology. Needs onboarding skills, complex scenarios, report interpretation, peer support.
Level 4 — Leadership
Service leader / educator
Local or regional leadership role. Needs comprehensive expertise across all systems plus complex case management.
