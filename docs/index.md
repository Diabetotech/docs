# Diabetotech — Open Diabetes Technology Reference

Free, peer-reviewed reference for healthcare professionals and people with diabetes worldwide.

## What you'll find here

Practical, device-focused education covering all major diabetes technologies:

- **CGM** — continuous glucose monitoring systems
- **Insulin Pumps** — Setup, programming, and daily management
- **AID Systems** — Automated insulin delivery, including open-source solutions
- **Smart Pens** — Connected multiple daily injection (MDI) technology

## How to use this site

Navigate by category or use the search bar to find specific topics.
Each module includes a video and a full transcript.
Content is updated regularly and developed independently — without commercial bias.
