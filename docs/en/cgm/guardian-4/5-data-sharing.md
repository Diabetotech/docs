## **<u>5. Data sharing</u>**

Welcome to this lesson on sharing data with the Guardian sensor.

In this video, we will explore how to share data with your healthcare provider, generate reports, and share data with family members or caregivers.

### ***5.1 Sharing data with your healthcare provider***

To share your data with your healthcare provider, Medtronic provides the CareLink platform. You can create a CareLink Personal account at carelink.minimed.eu or a CareLi<span class="mark">nk Clinic account at carelink.medtronic.eu.</span>

<span class="mark">If you are a healthcare professional, you can add your patients to CareLink Clinic by entering their name and date of birth.</span>

- You can then invite the patient to share their data by sending an email through CareLink.

- The patient can accept the invitation and enter the code from the email in their personal CareLink account settings.

When using the MiniMed Mobile app, it is linked to your personal CareLink account during the initial setup. If your personal CareLink account is connected to your hospital's account, your healthcare provider can view your data in real-time.

### ***5.2 Generating Reports***

Once your data is uploaded to CareLink, you can generate reports by selecting the glucose reports option. The process of creating and interpreting reports is explained in detail in the MiniMed 780G closed-loop system reports video.

### ***5.3 Sharing data with loved-ones***

To share Guardian 4 data with family members or caregivers, you have two options.

- Firstly, you can view CareLink data on your PC by creating a CareLink Personal account as a care partner. You will need to request access to the CareLink data of the user you want to track by entering their username. The user can then approve you as a caregiver through their own CareLink platform.

- Alternatively, if you use the MiniMed Mobile app, you can share your data with up to 5 caregivers using the CareLink Connect app. To begin, as a patient, you need to invite a follower to your MiniMed Mobile account. The follower must accept the invitation and install the CareLink Connect app to set up data sharing.

## Embracing these sharing features enhances collaboration, resilience, and informed decision-making for a more empowered journey towards better health.
