## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to this video module on the Guardian 4 continuous glucose monitor. Whether you are a healthcare professional or someone living with diabetes, this course will provide you with valuable insights and practical tips to make the most of the Guardian 4 system. Let's dive in!

The Guardian 4 is an advanced continuous glucose monitor primarily used in combination with the MiniMed 780G insulin pump. With a sensor lifespan of 7 days and a transmitter that can be reused for 1 year, the Guardian 4 offers an innovative solution for continuous glucose monitoring. You can conveniently access sensor data on your insulin pump or via the MiniMed Mobile app.

In previous videos, you learned about the reliability of glucose sensors, tips for handling sensor disconnection, and managing allergies. In this module, we will focus on the start-up education for the Guardian 4 sensor and share advanced tips and tricks for caregivers.

Please note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any changes to your therapy.

The videos in this course were created by Dr. Inge van Boxelaer, founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the <span class="mark">general St</span>-Lucas hospital in Ghent <span class="mark">in Belgium</span>. The content has been reviewed by by Dr. Henk-Jan Aanstoot, a pediatric endocrinologist and co-founder of Diabeter, and Sjoerd Aerts, a dutch individual living with type 1 diabetes who uses the Guardian 4 sensor in combination with the MiniMed 780G system. Their expertise and perspectives ensure that you receive honest opinions on the pros and cons o<span class="mark">f the Guardian 4 sensor.</span>

By the end of this course, you will have a comprehensive understanding of various aspects related to the Guardian 4 sensor. You will learn

- how to insert and remove the sensor correctly,

- start the sensor with the MiniMed 780G insulin pump,

- set alarms effectively,

- enable real-time data tracking for caregivers,

- and even explore the possibilities of connecting the Guardian 4 sensor to other devices.

As a healthcare professional, this knowledge will empower you to better guide your patients using the Guardian 4 sensor. And if you are a user of the Guardian 4 sensor, this course will equip you with a wealth of tips and tricks to enhance your experience. Get ready to embark on this exciting journey and make your life with the Guardian 4 even easier. Good luck!
