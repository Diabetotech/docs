## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

<span class="mark">Welcome to this module on the FreeStyle Libre 2 and FreeStyle Libre 2 Plus sensors! In this session, we’ll explore the details of the world’s most widely used glucose monitoring sensors.</span>

<span class="mark">The FreeStyle Libre 2 sensors combine a sensor and transmitter in one device, which can be easily placed on your body with a simple click.</span>

<span class="mark">The standard FreeStyle Libre 2 sensor functions as a flash glucose monitor. This means you can check your glucose levels by scanning the sensor with your phone or a dedicated reader. It offers a 14-day wear time, measures your glucose every minute, provides high and low glucose alerts, and is approved for people with diabetes aged 4 years and older.</span>

<span class="mark">In some countries, an advanced version of the FreeStyle LibreLink or Libre</span> **~~2~~** <span class="mark">app is available. This version doesn’t require manual scanning to view glucose readings, making it a true real-time continuous glucose monitor.</span>

<span class="mark">The FreeStyle Libre 2 Plus is a version of the FreeStyle Libre 2 sensor that can connect with automated insulin delivery systems like the Omnipod 5. It has a longer wear time of 15 days and is approved for use in children as young as 2 years old.</span>

This course is designed to empower both healthcare providers and individuals with diabetes to unlock the full potential of FreeStyle Libre 2. But before we dive in, remember that these videos do not serve as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any changes to your therapy.
