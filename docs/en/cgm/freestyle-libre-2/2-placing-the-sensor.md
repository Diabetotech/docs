### ***2.1 Supplies***

To begin, gather the necessary supplies: a sensor box and an alcohol swab. Inside the box, you'll find the sensor applicator and the sensor itself.

### ***2.2 Inserting the sensor***

Properly inserting the FreeStyle Libre 2 sensor involves the following steps:

- Start by washing your hands and selecting the spot on your upper arm where the sensor will be placed. Clean the chosen area wi<span class="mark">th water and soap and let it dry.</span>

- Open the sensor box by removing the lid and take off the cap from the sensor applicator.

- Place the sensor applicator inside the open box, aligning the marks on the applicator and the sensor box.

- Apply firm pressure to the sensor applicator until it stops.

- Position the loaded sensor applicator on the cleaned and dried spot on your upper arm, then push it firmly until the sensor is securely in place.

- After application, check the edges of the sensor patch to ensure proper adhesion.

### ***2.3 Removing the sensor***

To remove the FreeStyle Libre 2 sensor, gently and slowly pull it away from your skin. Dispose of the sensor in the appropriate bin.

### ***2.4 Do's & don’ts***

- For ease of placement on the upper arm, lift your arm and elbow to avoid excessive twisting.

- When placing the sensor, aim for a 90-degree angle.

<span class="mark">As you can see, placing the FreeStyle Libre 2 sensor is quite straightforward, and there’s little room for error. If you’re unsure about any step, an instructional manual is included in every box, so there’s no need to worry.</span>
