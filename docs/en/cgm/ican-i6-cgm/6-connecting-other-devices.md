## **<u>6. Connecting other devices</u>**

Welcome to this lesson on connecting your iCan i6 CGM to other devices. In this video, we'll outline sensor integration options with smartwatches, insulin pens, and insulin pumps. Let's start.

### 

### ***6.1 Smartwatches***

Right now, the iCan i6 CGM cannot connect directly to an Apple Watch or any Android smartwatch.

That said, the open-source app xDrip+ can be set up to work alongside the iCan app as a Companion App. This means if the iCan app is running on your phone, xDrip+ can capture the iCan app’s notifications or locally available data and use it to display your glucose readings on your Android smartwatch.

Please note that connecting via open-source apps is not supported by Sinocare and is not approved by the FDA or European authorities. Choosing to do so carries personal risk and responsibility. We do not recommend this approach, but we are disclosing it so you have a complete picture of what is available.

### ***6.2 Smart insulin pens***

The iCan i6 CGM does not support direct pairing with any smart insulin pen at this time.

### ***6.3 Smart insulin pumps***

# While the iCan CGM cannot be linked directly to any smart insulin pump at present, it is possible to connect an iCan CGM to AndroidAPS. However, the use of open-source applications is not supported by Sinocare and is not approved by the FDA or European regulatory authorities. Use is therefore at your own risk.
