## **<u>5. Data sharing (5.5 min)</u>**

Welcome to this lesson on sharing data with the Eversense 365 system.

In this session, you’ll learn how to share your data with your healthcare provider and loved ones and create glucose reports.

### ***5.1 Sharing data with your healthcare provider***

<span class="mark">The Eversense Data Management Software Program, or DMS Pro for short, is a free web-based app that enables patients, caregivers, and health care professionals to view and analyze glucose data that has been transmitted from the smart transmitter or the app.</span>

<span class="mark">Clinic Share is an optional feature on your app that allows you to either invite or accept an invitation from your healthcare provider's clinic using DMS Pro. To invite your healthcare provider, you'll need their DMS Pro Clinic ID number. Contact your clinic for this information.</span>

<span class="mark">From the Main Menu, go to "Share My Data” and “Clinic Share.” Then tap "Clinic Requests" and "Send Requests." Enter the DMS Pro clinic ID number, and tap "Add." Confirm the name of your clinic, then tap "Yes" to send the request. Tap "OK" to acknowledge the request has been sent. Your pending invitation will be displayed in red until your clinic accepts, upon which the name will change to black. To stop sharing data, tap on the clinic name and choose "Stop Sharing."</span>

<span class="mark">To receive a request, your clinic must use the email associated with your Eversense account. To check for pending requests, go to "Share My Data." Tap "Clinic Share," "Clinic Requests," and "Accept Requests." Tap on the clinic name in red, then tap "Accept" or "Decline" before confirming your selection. The clinic will appear on our "My Clinics" list.</span>

### ***5.2 Sharing data with your loved ones***

Using your Eversense 365 app, you also can invite your family and friends to join your "Circle." From the Share My Data screen, tap "My Circle" and "Invite to My Circle." Enter the email of the person you'd like to invite; a nickname is optional. You can also tap the "+" sign to select an email address from your Contacts. Tap "Send" then "OK" on the pop-up.

Your family member or friend will use the Eversense NOW Remote Monitoring App to monitor your CGM data. They can accept your invitation through the Eversense NOW app, or can follow the instructions in the email they receive. In your CGM app, their name will appear in red until they accept your invitation.

To remove someone from your circle, tap on their name, then tap "Remove" and confirm the pop-up notification. Finally, tap "OK" to confirm they are no longer in your Circle.

To temporarily stop sharing data with a member without removing them from your Circle, tap the member's name in the My Circle list to open the MEMBER screen. Then tap the Share My Data button to turn on/off data share with this member.

It’s important to note that you should not use glucose information displayed on the Eversense NOW App to make treatment decisions. The Eversense NOW App does not communicate directly with your sensor and/or smart transmitter. This makes the Eversense NOW App a secondary display of your CGM data that should not be used in place of the primary Eversense 365 App display.

### ***5.3 Creating reports***

Your Eversense 365 app automatically creates four reports you can view at any time, right in the app. These reports are the:

- Glucose Pie Chart, which shows what percent of your readings within a given time period are within, below or above your Glucose Target levels;

- Time in Range, which displays percentage of time you have spent in pre-set glucose ranges, based on standardized guidelines;

- Weekly Modal Summary, which provides a seven-day summary of your glucose information in a 24-hour line graph; and

- Glucose Statistics, which shows average, low, and high glucose values based on preset times.

To view your glucose reports on the app, tap Menu then Reports, and swipe to move across the different reports. You can also email each report by tapping the email icon in the top right-hand corner.

As a reminder, be sure to set your mobile device date and time correctly. The accuracy of the graphs and reports depends upon the accuracy of the date and time.

Furthermore, you can access reports on DMS Pro. To do this, go to https://us.eversensedms.com and enter your log-in information. On the left <span class="mark">navigation pa</span>ne, click on Reports to open the list of reports.

You may print or email your reports. Click on the printer icon to view and print your report, or click on the envelope icon to email them.

Located in the settings menu, REPORT SUMMARY settings allow you to set up a regularly-scheduled automated report that will be emailed to you. You can select how often the reports are sent, and which reports you want to receive.

In this video, you've learned how to use Eversense 365’s data sharing tools. Remember, a supportive network is vital for managing this journey. By sharing your CGM data, you empower your team with the actionable insights required to foster resilience and drive optimal health outcomes.
