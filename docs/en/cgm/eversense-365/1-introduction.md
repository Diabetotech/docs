## **<u>1. Introduction (3 min)</u>**

Welcome to this course on the Eversense 365 system. In this video, we'll explore the intricacies of the world's longest-lasting CGM. Let’s get started!

Measuring approximately 3.5 mm x 18.3 mm, the Eversense 365 sensor is placed under the skin of your upper arm through a tiny incision and secured with adhesive strips. After a 24-hour Warm-Up, the sensor works with an on-body transmitter to measure your glucose real-time. You can conveniently track your glucose readings updated every five minutes using the Eversense 365 app on your smartphone. The Eversense 365 sensor uses fluorescence to measure glucose in interstitial fluid. It is intended for individuals with diabetes 18 years or older and lasts for up to 1 year.

In the "Master Module on Glucose Sensors", we covered general information about glucose sensors, including insights on when to trust the sensor readings and tips for handling sensor disconnections and allergies. In the upcoming videos, we will focus on the start-up education for the Eversense 365 sensor and provide advanced tips and tricks for healthcare providers.

This course is designed to empower both healthcare providers and individuals with diabetes to unlock the full potential of the Eversense 365. But before we dive in, remember that these videos do not serve as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

These videos have been created by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the St-Lucas Hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content has been reviewed by Gary Scheiner, a Certified Diabetes Care and Education Specialist from Integrated Diabetes Services in the US. Their expertise and perspectives ensure that you receive honest opinions on the pros and cons of the Eversense 365 system.

Be assured, all the information provided here is completely independent from Senseonics, the sensor manufacturing company, ensuring a genuine perspective on the advantages and drawbacks of the Eversense 365.

By the end of this course, you will have gained a comprehensive understanding of how to:

- properly insert and remove the Eversense 365 sensor;

- start the sensor using the smart transmitter and the mobile app;

- set up alerts to receive timely notifications;

- share your glucose data with your healthcare provider and loved ones;

- connect the Eversense 365 sensor to other devices; and

- valuable tips and tricks to streamline your experience and enhance your daily diabetes management.

As a healthcare provider, you will gain the knowledge needed to guide individuals through using the Eversense 365 effectively, while users themselves will discover a wealth of tips and tricks to enhance their experience. Good luck on your journey with the Eversense 365!
