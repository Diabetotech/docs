## **Introduction**

Welcome <span class="mark">to this guide on the TouchCare Nano sensor, a continuous glucose monitor or CGM that can enhance your diabetes management. In this chapter, we will introduce the key features of the TouchCare Nano sensor and how to make the most of its benefits. Let's get started!</span>

<span class="mark">The TouchCare Nano CGM works alongside a PDM or dedicated mobile app and provides continuous glucose readings for up to 14 days per sensor. The reusable transmitter can be used repeatedly for up to 1 year and communicates with a PDM or smartphone via Bluetooth. The system measures your glucose levels every 2 minutes and displays the data conveniently on your smart device.</span>

<span class="mark">In other training materials, we have covered general glucose sensor usage, including when to rely on sensor data and tips for dealing with sensor issues or disconnections. In this guide, we will focus on the setup process for the TouchCare Nano CGM and provide practical guidance to help you use it effectively.</span>

<span class="mark">Please note that this guide is not a substitute for professional med</span>ical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any changes to your diabetes therapy.
