## **6. Connecting Other Devices (1.5 min)**

Welcome to this lesson on how to connect your TouchCare Nano sensor to other devices. In this section, we’ll explore which devices are compatible and how they can support your diabetes management.

### ***6.1 Smartwatches*** 

If you use your TouchCare Nano sensor with the EasyPatch or EasyTouch app connected to your PDM, you can enable pop-up alarms on your compatible phone and smartwatch, giving you quick access to important information.

<span class="mark">For iPhone users, a native Apple Watch integration is available through a dedicated Watch App, allowing you to view your sensor data in real time on your watch. However, if you use an Android phone or another type of smartwatch, real-time glucose display on your watch is currently not supported.</span>

### ***6.2 Smartpens*** 

The EasyPatch and EasyTouch apps are primarily designed for users of the TouchCare Nano Pump. At this time, they do not offer direct integration with smart insulin pens.

### ***6.3 Smart Insulin Pumps*** 

The TouchCare Nano sensor is not compatible with third-party insulin pumps or open-source closed-loop systems.

However, it pairs seamlessly with the TouchCare Nano 200 U or 300 U patch pump. When used together with the EasyPatch app or PDM, they offer a closed-loop system, automatically adjusting insulin delivery based on your CGM readings.

This makes the TouchCare Nano sensor and pump a powerful and convenient combination for managing diabetes.
