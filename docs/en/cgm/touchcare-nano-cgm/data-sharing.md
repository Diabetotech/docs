## **Data Sharing**

In this lesson, we will explain how to share your glucose data with healthcare providers and family members who support your diabetes management.

If you use the EasyPatch app, your data will automatically be sent to the Medtrum cloud every 2 minutes. If you use the PDM, you can connect the PDM to your phone using the EasyTouch app, so data is uploaded automatically from your PDM to the EasyTouch app every 2 minutes. This app mirrors the information on your PDM and also sends data to the cloud.

### ***5.1 Sharing Data with Your Healthcare Provider*** 

<span class="mark">Your healthcare provider can access your glucose data through the EasyView Pro platform, while patients and caregivers can use EasyView to monitor and review CGM data. Both EasyView (for users and caregivers) and EasyView Pro (for healthcare providers) accounts can be created free of charge.</span>

To get started:

- <span class="mark">Ensure that your EasyPatch or EasyTouch app is connected to your sensor and transmitting data.</span>

- <span class="mark">Your provider can add you as a patient using your registered email and invite you to connect.</span>

- <span class="mark">Once you accept the follow request, your healthcare provider will be able to follow you.</span>

### ***5.2 Creating and Viewing Reports*** 

Within the EasyPatch app, limited reports are available for the user. You can select daily sensor reports and trend analysis over 7, 30, or 90 days.

To see more comprehensive reports, you can log into the EasyView platform. Here, multiple reports are available:

- <span class="mark">Dashboard, which gives you an overview and averages on the patient's data.</span>

- <span class="mark">Daily summary, which gives you a detailed overview of a selected date.</span>

- <span class="mark">Day by day overview, which gives you either the last 7 or 14 days worth of data on one page.</span>

- <span class="mark">Sensor overlay, which gives you the last seven days worth of CGM readings all on top of each other.</span>

- <span class="mark">Trend analysis, which gives you details of both pump and CGM averages.</span>

- <span class="mark">Events, which gives you the history of every event that has been logged through the patient's phone or PDM handset.</span>

- <span class="mark">Logbook, which gives you details of the last 7 or 14 days worth of data in regards to BG levels, carbohydrate intake, bolus dosages along with further information.</span>

- <span class="mark">Alert, which gives you a history of every alert that has appeared on the mobile phone or PDM handset, as well as the pump.</span>

- <span class="mark">And finally Device Settings Review, which gives you details on the settings of the senssor.</span>

Reports can be printed, downloaded, or shared electronically, helping you and your care team make informed decisions.

### ***5.3 Sharing Data with Family and Caregivers*** 

Family and Caregivers can use the EasyFollow app. This app allows you to share your <span class="mark">real-time glucose data with an unlimited amount of caregivers. Here’s how:</span>

- <span class="mark">To connect, caregivers can find the user account by entering their email address in the EasyFollow app or on the EasyView website.</span>

- To accept follow requests, the user then goes to Main Menu → Settings → Account Security → Remote View Permission in the EasyPatch app, or to Profile and Connections on the EasyView website.

- <span class="mark">Once connected, caregivers can view real-time glucose readings, trend arrows, and receive alerts, with the ability to adjust notification preferences while the user maintains control over shared data.</span>

Sharing data with healthcare providers and loved ones creates a support system that enhances diabetes management. By taking this step, you ensure that those who care for you can help keep you safe and supported.
