## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to this video on the Dexcom G7, a continuous glucose monitor that can

The Dexcom G7 combines a sensor and transmitter into a single device, providing continuous glucose monitoring that lasts for 10 days with a 12 hour grace period. This advanced sensor measures your blood sugar levels every 5 minutes, and you can easily access this data on your compatible mobile phone or on a Dexcom G7 receiver. greatly improve your diabetes management. In this chapter, we will explore the key features of the Dexcom G7 and how to maximize its benefits. Let's dive in!

<span class="mark">In some cases, the Dexcom G7 sensor can also be linked to certain automated insulin delivery systems for enhanced diabetes management.</span>

<span class="mark">The standard Dexcom G7 has a warm-up time of 30 minutes.</span>

<span class="mark">In the United States, an updated version of the Dexcom G7 is available with a wear time of up to 15 days, plus a 12-hour grace period. The standard Dexcom G7 is approved for use in people aged 2 years and older, whereas the 15-day Dexcom G7 is currently approved for adults aged 18 years and older.</span>
