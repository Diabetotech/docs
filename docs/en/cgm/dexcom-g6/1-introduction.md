## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to this video on the Dexcom G6, a continuous glucose monitor that can greatly improve your diabetes management. In this chapter, we will explore the key features of the Dexcom G6 and how to maximize its benefits. Let's dive in!

The Dexcom G6 is primarily used in conjunction with an insulin pump and offers continuous glucose monitoring for up to 10 days. The transmitter, on the other hand, can be reused for a period of up to 3 months. The Dexcom G6 sensor measures your glycemia every 5 minutes. You can conveniently access your sensor data through your compatible mobile phone or, in some cases, via your insulin pump. Additionally, there is a Dexcom G6 receiver available for alternative data retrieval.

In the "Master Module on Glucose Sensors", we covered general information about glucose sensors, including insights on when to trust the sensor readings and tips for handling sensor disconnections and allergies. In the upcoming videos, we will focus on the start-up education for the standalone Dexcom G6 sensor and provide advanced tips and tricks for healthcare providers.

Please note that these videos are not intended as a substitute for medical advice. It is always essential to consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

The content of these videos has been developed by Dr. Inge van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the St-Lucas hospital in Ghent. The materials have been reviewed by prof. dr. Laurent Crenier, Head of the Diabetology Clinic at Hôpital Erasme in Brussels, and Cathy Van de Moortele, a passionate advocate for people living with diabetes and blogger about her personal journey with type 1 diabetes. <span class="mark">Their expertise and perspectives ensure that you receive honest opinions on the pros and cons of the Dexcom G6 sensor.</span>

By the end of these videos, you will have gained a comprehensive understanding of:

- How to properly insert and remove the Dexcom G6 sensor

- The process of starting the sensor using the Dexcom G6 app

- Setting up alarms to receive timely notifications

- Enabling real-time tracking of sensor data for your family and caregivers

- Connecting the Dexcom G6 sensor to other devices,

- And valuable tips and tricks to streamline your experience and enhance your daily diabetes management.

This knowledge will empower healthcare providers to guide individuals using the Dexcom G6 sensor effectively, and it will equip users of the Dexcom G6 sensor with valuable tips and tricks to enhance their experience. Good luck on your journey with the Dexcom G6!
