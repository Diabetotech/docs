## **<span class="mark"><u>5. Data sharing</u></span>**

<span class="mark">Welcome to this lesson on sharing data with the Simplera sensor.</span>

<span class="mark">In this video, we will explore how to share data with your healthcare provider, generate reports, and share data with family members or caregivers.</span>

### ***<span class="mark">5.1 Sharing data with your healthcare provider</span>***

<span class="mark">To share your data with your healthcare provider, Medtronic provides the CareLink platform. As a user or a family member, you can create a CareLink Personal account at carelink.minimed.com. As a healthcare provider, you can create a CareLink Clinic account at carelink.medtronic.eu, or via carelink.medtronic.com if you live in the US.</span>

<span class="mark">If you are a healthcare professional, you can add your patients to CareLink Clinic by entering their name and date of birth.</span>

- <span class="mark">You can then invite the patient to share their data by sending an email through CareLink.</span>

- <span class="mark">The patient can accept the invitation and enter the code from the email in their personal CareLink account or via the MiniMed Mobile app.</span>

<span class="mark">When using the Simplera app or MiniMed Mobile app, these are linked to your personal CareLink account during the initial setup. If your personal CareLink account is connected to your hospital's account, your healthcare provider can view your data in real-time.</span>

### ***<span class="mark">5.2 Generating Reports</span>***

<span class="mark">Once your data is uploaded to CareLink, you can generate reports by selecting the reports option. The process of creating and interpreting reports is explained in detail in the MiniMed 780G automated insulin delivery system reports video.</span>

### ***<span class="mark">5.3 Sharing data with loved-ones</span>***

<span class="mark">To share Simplera data with family members or caregivers, you have two options.</span>

- <span class="mark">Firstly, you can view CareLink data on your PC by creating a CareLink Personal account as a care partner. You will need to request access to the CareLink data of the user you want to track by entering their username. The user can then approve you as a caregiver through their own CareLink platform.</span>

- <span class="mark">Alternatively, if you use the Simplera app or the MiniMed Mobile app, you can share your data with up to 5 care partners using the CareLink Connect app. To begin, care partners need to download the CareLink Connect app on their compatible mobile phone. They must then create a care partner account and send a follow request by entering the username of the patient they wish to follow. The patient must accept this request through their MiniMed Mobile or Simplera app, or directly via their CareLink account on a computer.</span>

<span class="mark">Embracing these sharing features enhances collaboration, resilience, and informed decision-making for a more empowered journey towards better health.</span>
