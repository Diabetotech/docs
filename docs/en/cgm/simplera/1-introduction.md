## **<span class="mark"><u>1. Introduction</u></span>**

<span class="mark">Welcome to this video module on the Simplera continuous glucose monitor. Whether you are a healthcare professional or someone living with diabetes, this course will provide you with valuable insights and practical tips to make the most of the Simplera system. Let's dive in!</span>

<span class="mark">The Simplera sensor is a glucose sensor and transmitter in one device, and has a lifespan of maximum 7 days. It measures your glucose every 5 minutes. There are 2 different types of Simplera sensors. The Simplera CGM can be used in combination with the Simplera app, the InPen and InPen app, while the Simplera Sync sensor can be used with the MiniMed 780G insulin pump and MiniMed Mobile app. It’s important to note that the Simplera Sync sensor is not compatible with the Simplera app, and that the regular Simplera CGM is not compatible with the MiniMed 780G pump.</span>

<span class="mark">In previous videos, you learned about the reliability of glucose sensors, tips for handling sensor disconnection, and managing allergies. In this module, we will focus on the start-up education for the Simplera sensor and share advanced tips and tricks for caregivers. This course focuses on the Simplera CGM and Simplera Sync sensors, though for simplicity, we solely use the name Simplera. Please note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any changes to your therapy.</span>

<span class="mark">The videos in this course were created by Dr. Inge van Boxelaer, founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the St-Lucas hospital in Ghent, Belgium. The content has been reviewed by Dr. Henk-Jan Aanstoot, founder of Diabeter, which are diabetes treatment centers in the Netherlands, and Sjoerd Aerts, a user of the MiniMed 780G system and administrator of the dutch FaceBook page about the MiniMed 780G. Their expertise and perspectives ensure that you receive honest opinions on the pros and cons of different sensors.</span>

<span class="mark">By the end of this course, you will have a comprehensive understanding of various aspects related to the Simplera sensor. You will learn how to insert and remove the sensor correctly, start the sensor with the Simplera app and the MiniMed 780G insulin pump, set alarms effectively, enable real-time data tracking for caregivers, and even explore the possibilities of connecting the Simplera sensor to other devices.</span>

<span class="mark">As a healthcare professional, this knowledge will empower you to better guide your patients using the Simplera sensor. And if you are a user of the Simplera sensor, this course will equip you with a wealth of tips and tricks to enhance your experience. Get ready to embark on this exciting journey and make your life with the Simplera even easier. Good luck!</span>
