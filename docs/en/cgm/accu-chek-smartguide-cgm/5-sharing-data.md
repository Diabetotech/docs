## **<u>5. Sharing data</u>**

Welcome to this lesson on sharing data with the Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM. In this video, we’ll explain how you can share your sensor data with your healthcare provider and generate reports with the Predict app. Let's jump right in.

### ***5.1 Sharing data with your healthcare provider*** 

<span class="mark">The Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM is fully integrated with the Accu-Chek Care platform, giving healthcare professionals continuous access to detailed and therapy-relevant CGM data. The SmartGuide app automatically syncs all readings to the Accu-Chek cloud, allowing real-time monitoring through the Accu-Chek Care platform.</span>

<span class="mark">As a healthcare provider, you can request access to the Accu-Chek Care platform through your local Roche Diagnostics representative. Once your access is approved, you can log in at</span> <span class="mark">[<u>https://care.rochediabetes.com</u>](https://care.rochediabetes.com) for international users or</span> <span class="mark">[<u>https://hcp.myrdcp.com</u>](https://hcp.myrdcp.com) for users in the United States.</span>

<span class="mark">When you log into Accu-Chek Care as a healthcare provider, you will first see the BGM view. This default view presents a high-level summary of your patients’ data from the past 14 days. Each row displays personal and health information about a specific patient. For more details about hypo and hyper events, click the drop-down arrow at the end of the patient row. Use the search field to find patients by name or ID. Click the logo in the upper left corner to clear all filters and reset search results. Select results per page to change the number of patients displayed at a time.Click “Sort by” to change the order by name or patient results. Click “Filters” to refine results by category. Use arrows by column headings to view in ascending or descending order. Click a patient’s name to open the Patient View. Return to Patient Management by clicking the logo in the upper left corner. Click CGM in the top menu bar to view Patient Management for CGM. This view shows data for patients using a CGM, such as percentage of CGM active time and glucose management indicator. Click the patient’s name to open Patient View.</span>

<span class="mark">To allow your healthcare provider to access your CGM data, you must first be logged in to your Accu-Chek account. From the SmartGuide app Home screen, tap Menu, go to Account and select Manage Account; this will take you to your Accu-Chek account page. In your account, open the Sharing tab and choose Manage Shared Data, then select Generate code. The code that appears is case-sensitive and can be shared with your healthcare provider. Once the connection has been set up successfully, the link to your healthcare provider will be shown in your Accu-Chek account.</span>

<span class="mark">As a healthcare provider, you can add a new patient by tapping Create new patient and entering the sharing code. After you confirm the code, the patient’s information will auto-populate, and you can complete the creation of the new patient profile.</span>

<span class="mark">In the patient settings, you can update the patient’s account information, health and personal information and account configuration. You can adjust the meal-related time blocks, edit the glucose target ranges and see a list of all devices associated with the patient. Deactivating a patient will remove them from the current patient list.</span>

### ***5.2 Creating reports***

<span class="mark">To view reports as a healthcare provider, sign in to the platform. You will automatically arrive in the Patient Management view. To quickly narrow your list to patients using CGM therapy, select CGM from the menu bar. Click a patient’s name to go to Patient View. The CGM view displays graphs and statistics for the patient’s available CGM data, including: Time In Range, the standardized ambulatory glucose profile and daily profiles. Click information icons to learn more about each graph. Use arrows to expand the graph. Click legend to view graphed elements. Daily Glucose Profiles can also be expanded to show more details. Click the back arrow to return to the default CGM View.</span>

<span class="mark">If you don’t have access to Accu-Chek Care webplatform, the Accu-Chek SmartGuide Predict app also gives you the option of creating and sharing your Ambulatory Glucose Profile and your detected Patterns for a 14-day time period. In the app Menu, select "Export report" and choose the desired time interval. Select to export the AGP, patterns, or both. Attach the PDF to an email and send it to your healthcare provider. While you can view patterns in the app, the full AGP report is only available as a PDF.</span>

### ***5.3 Sharing data with your family or loved one***

<span class="mark">Until now, there is no way to share Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM data in real time with a family member or caregiver. There is no follow app available for this.</span>
