## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to this module on the Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM! In the videos ahead, we’ll explore the key features of this sensor and how it works with an AI-enabled predictive algorithm to support proactive diabetes management.

The Accu-Chek SmartGuide solution consists of three elements: the sensor, the SmartGuide app, and the Predict app. The sensor, which has a diameter of 3 cm and weighs about 5 grams, is placed on the back of your upper arm using an all-in-one applicator and lasts for up to 14 days. After a one-hour warm-up, it sends real-time glucose readings every five minutes to the SmartGuide app on your smartphone via Bluetooth. The Predict app, with its AI-powered algorithm, then uses these glucose values and other available information to detect your glucose patterns and estimate your levels over the next 30 minutes, 2 hours, and during the night, helping you stay ahead of potential changes. The Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM solution is intended for individuals 18 years of age and older.

In our "Master Module on Glucose Sensors", we covered the essential principles of CGM use, from appropriate reliance on sensor readings and management of issues such as disconnections and allergic reactions. We now turn to in-depth training on the Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM and share practical strategies for getting the most from the device in daily life.

We’ve created this module to help healthcare providers and individuals with diabetes use the Accu-Chek SmartGuide CGM as effectively as possible. However, please remember that these videos should not be taken as medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before adjusting your treatment plan.
