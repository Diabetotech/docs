## **<u>1. Introduction (2.5 min)</u>**

Welcome to this module on the Instinct CGM! Throughout this video series, we’ll cover everything you need to know about this sensor designed exclusively for MiniMed™ systems. Let’s get started.

The Instinct sensor measures 21 by 21 by 3 millimeters and weighs 1 g. It is inserted on the back of your upper arm using an all-in-one applicator. After a one-hour warm-up, it measures your glucose levels in real time and delivers readings to your insulin pump every five minutes. You can then view your sensor and pump data on both your MiniMed™ 780G insulin pump and your smartphone via the MiniMed™ Mobile app. The sensor lasts for up to 15 days. It is intended for people with diabetes 7 and old<span class="mark">er in the US, but age indication may differ in other regions.</span>
