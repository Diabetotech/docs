## **<u>5. Sharing data</u>**

Welcome to this lesson on sharing data with the Instinct CGM. In this video, we’ll walk you through sharing your sensor glucose data with your healthcare provider and loved ones. We’ll also discuss viewing and creating your glucose reports with the CareLink<sup>TM</sup> system. Let’s start.

### ***<span class="mark">5.1 Sharing data with your healthcare provider</span>***

<span class="mark">To share your data with your healthcare provider, Medtronic provides the CareLink platform. As a user or a family member, you can create a CareLink Personal account at carelink.minimed.com. As a healthcare provider, you can create a CareLink Clinic account at carelink.medtronic.eu, or via carelink.medtronic.com if you live in the US.</span>

<span class="mark">If you are a healthcare professional, you can add your patients to CareLink Clinic by entering their name and date of birth.</span>

- <span class="mark">You can then invite the patient to share their data by sending an email through CareLink.</span>

- <span class="mark">The patient can accept the invitation and enter the code from the email in their personal CareLink account or via the MiniMed Mobile app.</span>

<span class="mark">When using the Instinct app or MiniMed Mobile app, these are linked to your personal CareLink account during the initial setup. If your personal CareLink account is connected to your hospital's account, your healthcare provider can view your data in real-time.</span>

### ***<span class="mark">5.2 Generating Reports</span>***

<span class="mark">Once your data is uploaded to CareLink, you can generate reports by selecting the reports option. The process of creating and interpreting reports is explained in detail in the MiniMed 780G automated insulin delivery system reports video.</span>

### ***<span class="mark">5.3 Sharing data with loved-ones</span>***

<span class="mark">To share Instinct data with family members or caregivers, you have two options.</span>

- <span class="mark">Firstly, you can view CareLink data on your PC by creating a CareLink Personal account as a care partner. You will need to request access to the CareLink data of the user you want to track by entering their username. The user can then approve you as a caregiver through their own CareLink platform.</span>

- <span class="mark">Alternatively, if you use the MiniMed Mobile app, you can share your data with up to 5 care partners using the CareLink Connect app. To begin, care partners need to download the CareLink Connect app on their compatible mobile phone. They must then create a care partner account and send a follow request by entering the username of the patient they wish to follow. The patient must accept this request through their MiniMed Mobile app, or directly via their CareLink account on a computer.</span>

### ***5.4 Tips***

Take note of the following guidelines on sharing your Instinct sensor data.

- For information to be sent to the CareLink™ Connect app, the MiniMed<sup>TM</sup> Mobile app must be:

  - within 6 meters (or 20 feet) of the pump

  - have internet connection

  - be logged into CareLink™ Personal on the app, and

  - have Sync to CareLink™ turned on.

- Both Pump User and Care Partner must be logged into Carelink™ Personal for Carelink™ Connect App to receive data.

- One MiniMed™ Mobile app user can have their data followed by up to 5 CareLink™ Connect app users, and one CareLink™ Connect app user can follow up to 5 MiniMed™ Mobile app users.

- If nothing happens when you try to generate reports, you may need to disable your browser’s pop-up blocker. Call Medtronic Customer Service if the problem persists.

# In this video, we looked at multiple options for sharing your Instinct CGM data. Using these features helps you collaborate more effectively with your care team, so you can make prompt, data-driven decisions for improved health outcomes.
