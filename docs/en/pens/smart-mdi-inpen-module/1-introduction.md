### **1. Introduction**

Welcome to this module on the Smart MDI system, a connected solution for people with diabetes using multiple daily injections.

This system comb<span class="mark">ines the Bluetooth-enabled InPen with a powerful mobile app, allowing users to:</span>

- <span class="mark">Calculate accurate insulin bolus doses by choosing one of the 3 possibilities: Carb counting, Meal estimation or Fixed Dose</span>

- <span class="mark">Log insulin administration automatically</span>

- <span class="mark">Integrate glucose data from the Simplera CGM</span>

- <span class="mark">And share information with care teams or caregivers</span>

<span class="mark">Please note that while the system can be connected to the Guardian 4 sensor, this module will not cover that specific connectivity.</span>

<span class="mark">Also, be aware that Simplera CGM and Simplera Sync are two distinct sensors:</span>

- <span class="mark">Simplera CGM is intended for use with the current Smart MDI system.</span>

- <span class="mark">Simplera Sync is designed for integration with the MiniMed 780G insulin pump.</span>

<span class="mark">Like insulin pumps and CGM systems, this Smart MDI solution is designed to improve safety, confidence, and outcomes in diabetes care. Key advantages include:</span>

- <span class="mark">Reliable tracking of insulin on board</span>

- <span class="mark">An integrated bolus calculator for both mealtime and correction insulin</span>

- <span class="mark">Smart actionable insulin reminders</span>

- <span class="mark">And insightful data reports for both CGM and insulin data\
  </span>

<span class="mark">In this module, you will learn to:</span>

- <span class="mark">Set up and configure the Smart MDI system</span>

- <span class="mark">Use it effectively in daily practice</span>

- <span class="mark">Interpret the dedicated InPen reports</span>

- <span class="mark">And learn from a real-world case example\
  </span>

<span class="mark">This course was developed by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer,</span> endocrinologist at the general Saint-Lucas hospital in Ghent and founder of Diabetotech. It has been peer-reviewed by Dr<span class="mark">. Liesbeth Van Huffel, an endocrinologist in the OLV Hospital in Aalst, and Christiane Segers, a person living with type 1 diabetes who actively uses this Smart MDI system.</span>

It is important to note that the information presented is independent of device manufacturers and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult your diabetes care team before making treatment changes.

Over the next 30 minutes, you’ll gain the knowledge and skills <span class="mark">needed to use or support people using the Smart MDI system confidently and effectively. Let’s begin.</span>
