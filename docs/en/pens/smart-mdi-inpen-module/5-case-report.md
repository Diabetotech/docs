### **5. Case report**

To conclude this module, we present a real-world example of how the Smart MDI system — combining the InPen smart insulin pen and Simplera CGM — is experienced by a person living with type 1 diabetes.

The review, titled “I Tried Smart Insulin Pen: Simplera Smart MDI Review,” is available on YouTube and features a person with type 1 diabetes who transitioned from an insulin pump to the Smart MDI system.

[<u>Watch the video</u>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T8YMFfuwMx0)

The reviewer shares several positive aspects of the system such as reduced mental burden, helpful reminders, improved glycemic awareness and greater confidence. While the system was generally well received, the reviewer highlighted a few areas for improvement.

- Managing two separate apps — one for glucose (Simplera) and one for insulin (InPen) — was occasionally cumbersome.

- And full functionality is only achieved when both the Simplera CGM and InPen are used together.

Real-world testimonials like this offer important context for both healthcare providers and people with diabetes. Hearing directly from users helps clinicians understand not just the technical capabilities of these tools, but their psychological and behavioral impact.
