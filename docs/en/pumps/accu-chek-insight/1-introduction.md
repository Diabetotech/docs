## **<u>1. Introduction (2 min)</u>**

Welcome to this module about the Accu-Chek Insight insulin p<span class="mark">ump! Although this pump is no longer being commercialised, some people are still using it.</span>

In previous general insulin pump videos, you learned the fundamentals of how insulin pumps work, setting basal and bolus insulin, changing insulin and infusion sets, and handling alarms.

In the following videos, we will take you through the complete start-up education for the Accu-Chek Insight pump. It's important to note that these videos <span class="mark">are not intended as</span> medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

Dr. Inge <span class="mark">V</span>an Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the <span class="mark">general St-Lucas hospital in Ghent, has created these informative videos. The content has</span> been reviewed by Professor Dr. Laurent Crenier, an endocrinologist at Erasmus Hospital in Brussels, Belgium, and Renate Groenendijk, a user of the Diabeloop-Insight system and moderator of the dutch Diabeloop FaceBook group. These individuals have no financial interests in Roche, ensuring that the information provided is completely unbiased and independent.

By the end of these videos, you will have a clear understanding of:

- How the Accu-Chek Insight pump functions

- Setting basal and bolus insulin levels on the pump

- Changing the insulin PumpCart and infusion set

- And managing alarms and responding appropriately

Whether you are a caregiver or a user, this knowledge will empower you to better guide individuals using the Accu-Chek Insight pump. As a user, you'll gain valuable tips and tricks to enhance your experience with the pump. Remember, your diabetes journey can be more manageable with the right tools and knowledge.

We wish you the best of luck as you embark on this insightful video program. Let's dive in and explore the world of the Accu-Chek Insight insulin pump together!
