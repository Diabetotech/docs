## 1. Introduction (2 min)

Welcome to the twiist Pump module!

In this session, you'll learn how to operate the twiist pump confidently and effectively, whether for your own diabetes management or to support others.

Before we begin, we recommend watching our general insulin pump videos. These explain the basics of basal and bolus setup, infusion site management, and alarm handling — essential knowledge for understanding any pump system.

We'll now focus on the twiist Pump, part of the twiist Automated Insulin Delivery (AID) system. This innovative pump connects seamlessly with compatible glucose sensors and can automate insulin delivery using the integrated twiist Loop algorithm.

In this module, we'll guide you through the full setup process and daily operation of the twiist pump. It's designed for both healthcare professionals supporting twiist AID users and individuals preparing to start with the system.

Please note that the information in this module is for educational purposes only and should not be taken as medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

The videos were produced by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the AZ St-Lucas hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content was reviewed by Gary Scheiner, a Certified Diabetes Care and Education Specialist from Integrated Diabetes Services in the US. Their expertise and personal experiences ensure that the information presented in the videos is comprehensive and unbiased.

By the end of this module, you'll know:

* How the twiist pump works
* How to set basal and bolus insulin delivery
* How to replace the insulin cassette (reservoir) and infusion set
* And what to do when the pump gives alarms or alerts

Whether you're a healthcare provider or a user, by the end of this module, you will gain practical tips, step-by-step guidance, and the confidence to manage the twiist system effectively.
