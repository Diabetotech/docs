## **<u>1. Introduction (2 min)</u>**

Welcome to our focused exploration of the Tandem Mobi insuli<span class="mark">n pu</span>mp!

By now, you've gained a solid understanding of insulin pump functionality through our previous general insulin pump videos. You've delved into fundamentals of how insulin pumps work, setting basal and bolus insulin, changing insulin and infusion sets, and handling alarms.

Now, we turn our attention to the Tandem Mobi Insulin Pump. In this module, you'll walk through a comprehensive set-up education for this advanced pump. These explanations will benefit healthcare professionals guiding individuals using the Tandem Mobi Insulin Pump with Control-IQ technology and offer support for those initiating the use of the Tandem Mobi Insulin pump.

It is important to note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before adjusting your therapy.
