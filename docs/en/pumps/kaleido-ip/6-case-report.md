## **<u>6. Case report</u>**

<span class="mark">Welcome to this final part of the module about the Kaleido insulin pump!</span> You've gained valuable insights into its features and functions, showing your dedication to managing diabetes effectively. Now, let's take a look at a real-life review of the Kaleido pump.

In this video review by Sanne and Anne, they discuss their experience with the Kaleido pump. If you understand Dutch, you can watch the video by clicking on the link provided. If not, we can summarize it for you.

[<u>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QCxFEdR9fuI</u>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QCxFEdR9fuI)

Sanne and Anne appreciated the pump's compact size, lack of tubing, and attractive design compared to other insulin pumps. They found it convenient for exercising and discreet bolusing via the handset.

However, they noted a few drawbacks. The handset requires daily charging, and occasionally, the pump may need to be recharged between sessions, possibly due to initial incomplete charging. They also mentioned that the pump patch can loosen, especially with frequent removal and reattachment, <span class="mark">and that they needed to carry a lot of equipment due to the many different parts of the pump.</span>
