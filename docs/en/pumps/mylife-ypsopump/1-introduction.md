**<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to our comprehensive overview of the mylife YpsoPump!

<span class="mark">This insulin pump serves as a key component of the mylife Loop automated insulin delivery system and is typically used alongside a Dexcom G6 or FreeStyle Libre3 sensor. The mylife YpsoPump distinguishes itself from other tubed pumps with its compact size and ease of use, facilitated by its use of prefilled insulin PumpCarts. This pump also employs a unique system of icons, which, while initially may require some adjustment, are ultimately quite intuitive.</span>

<span class="mark">In previous general insulin pump videos, you learned the fundamentals of how insulin pumps work, setting basal and bolus insulin, changing insulin and infusion sets, and handling alarms.</span>

<span class="mark">In the upcoming videos, we'll walk you through the entire start-up procedure for the mylife YpsoPump, providing detailed guidance specific to this particular pump. It's important to note that these videos are not intended as medical advice, so always consult your healthcare provider before making any changes to your therapy.</span>

<span class="mark">These videos are brought to you by Dr. Inge van Boxelaer, founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the general St-Lucas hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content has been reviewed by Daniella Struijk, a diabetes educator from the Netherlands who uses the mylife Loop system herself and Tom Vesely from Germany, an individual living with type 1 diabetes who reviewed mylife Loop on his</span> TypeOne Talks YouTube channel. Their expertise and personal experience contribute to the comprehensive and reliable information provided in the videos.

Upon completing these videos, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of:

- How the mylife YpsoPump functions

- How to adjust basal and bolus insulin using the mylife YpsoPump

- How to change the insulin PumpCart and infusion set

- And what to do if an alarm on the pump is triggered.

This will empower you, whether you're a caregiver or a user, to better navigate the YpsoPump system and discover all the helpful tips and tricks to make life with the mylife YpsoPump smoother and simpler.

Best of luck on this journey!
