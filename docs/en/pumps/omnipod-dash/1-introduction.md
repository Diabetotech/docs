## **<u>1. Introduction</u>** 

Welcome to this module on the Omnipod DASH insulin pump! In this video, we will explore the features and operation of the Omnipod DASH. This patchpump is usually used as a separate manual pump, but can <span class="mark">also be used in open-source automated insulin delivery systems such as AndroidAPS, DIY Loop, iAPS or Trio.</span>

<span class="mark">It's important to note that the Omnipod DASH is distinct from the Omnipod 5. The Omnipod 5 is Insulet’s commercial closed-loop system linked to a Dexcom or FreeStyle Libre sensor. While the external appearance of the Pods is similar between the two models, the software and functionality differ significantly. Additionally, the Omnipod 5's Controller is slightly larger and contains different software compared to the PDM of the Omnipod DASH.</span>

This video specifically focuses on the Omnipod DASH insulin patch pump, providing valuable insights into its usage and functionality.

In the general module on insulin pumps, you have already learned about the fundamentals of insulin pumps, such as their operation, setting basal and bolus insulin, changing insulin and infusion sets, and managing alarms.

In the upcoming videos, we will showcase the Omnipod DASH in action, covering the entire onboarding education for this patch pump. However, please keep in mind that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

These videos have been created by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the St-Lucas Hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content has been reviewed by Dr. Sarah Siegelaar, an endocrinologist at Amsterdam UMC in the Netherlands, as well as Veerle Kerckvoorde, an individual living with type 1 diabetes who uses the Omnipod DASH in combination with AndroidAPS. Their contributions ensure that the information provided is unbiased and independent.

By the end of these videos, you will have a comprehensive understanding of how the Omnipod DASH insulin pump works, how to set basal and bolus insulin, how to change the insulin Pod, and what actions to take in case of an alarm.

Whether you are a caregiver looking to guide individuals using the Omnipod DASH or a user preparing for a successful start-up, these videos will equip you with valuable knowledge, along with practical tips and tricks to enhance your experience with the Omnipod DASH.

We wish you the best of luck on your journey with the Omnipod DASH insulin pump!
