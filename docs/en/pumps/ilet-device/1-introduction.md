## **<u>1. Introduction</u>** 

Welcome to this informative video on the iLet Device! This insulin pump is used in conjunction with iLet Dosing Decision Software, which combines it with a Dexcom G6 or G7 sensor for advanced diabetes management.

In previous general insulin pump videos, you learned the fundamentals of how insulin pumps work, setting basal and bolus insulin, changing insulin and infusion sets, and handling alarms.

In the following videos, we will specifically focus on the iLet insulin pump, providing a comprehensive start-up education for this innovative pump. These explanations will benefit healthcare professionals guiding individuals using the iLet Device and offer support for those initiating the use of the iLet Device. Please note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. It is crucial to consult with your healthcare provider before making any therapy adjustments.

The videos were produced by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the St-Lucas hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content was reviewed by <span class="mark">Dr. Diana Isaacs, the Director of Education in Training in Diabetes Technology at Cleveland Clinic Diabetes Center and Chelsea Lugone, a certified diabetes educator living with type 1 diabetes in the US who has firsthand experience with the iLet Device. Their expertise and personal experiences ensu</span>re that the information presented in the videos is comprehensive and unbiased.

By watching these videos, you will gain a clear understanding of:

- How the iLet Device operates

- How to setup the pump to start dosing basal insulin and give meal boluses

- How to change the insulin reservoir and infusion set

- And what actions to take in case of pump alarms

As a healthcare professional, these videos will equip you with the knowledge to better guide individuals using the iLet Device. For iLet Device users, this resource will assist in preparing for a successful start with the pump, while also providing valuable tips and tricks to make life with the iLet Device even more manageable.

Wishing you the best of luck on your journey with the iLet Device, whether as a healthcare professional or an individual with diabetes.
