## **<u>1. Introduction (2.5 min)</u>**

Welcome to our focused exploration of the MiniMed 780G pump!

<span class="mark">This insulin tubed pump is a key component in the MiniMed 780G automated insulin delivery system, where it works in collaboration with a Guardian, Simplera Sync or Instinct sensor.</span>

<span class="mark">By now, you've gained a solid understanding of insulin pump functionality through our general videos. You've delved into setting basal and bolus insulin, handling infusion sites and managing alarms.</span>

<span class="mark">Now, we're turning our lens towards the MiniMed 780G insulin pump. You'll walk through a comprehensive setup tutorial for this widely used tubed pump, providing an accessible guide for healthcare professionals aiding MiniMed 780G users, and supportive resources for those starting their journey with this pump. It's important to note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before adjusting your therapy.</span>

<span class="mark">This module was developed by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, founder of Diabetotech and endocrinologist at the general St-Lucas hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content was reviewed by Dr. Henk-Jan Aanstoot, founder of Diabeter, which are diabetes treatment centers in the Netherlands, and Sjoerd Aerts, a user of the MiniMed 780G system and administrator of the dutch FaceBook page about the MiniMed 780G. Here you can see their disclosures. Their expertise and perspectives ensure that you receive honest opinions on the pros and cons of the MiniMed 780G insulin pump.</span>

By the end of this series, you'll have a comprehensive understanding of the MiniMed 780G's operation, setting basal and bolus insulin, changing the insulin reservoir and infusion set, and handling pump alarms. This will enhance your ability as a healthcare provider to assist MiniMed 780G users, or as a user, to prepare for a successful setup. We'll also provide you with handy tips and tricks to make your life with the MiniMed 780G easier.

Best of luck on your journey with the MiniMed 780G!
