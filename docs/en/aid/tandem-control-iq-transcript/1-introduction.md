## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to this module on Tandem Control-IQ technology, one of the most widely used automated insulin delivery systems, particularly in the US. In this comprehensive guide, we will delve into the intricacies of Tandem Control-IQ technology, providing you with a step-by-step understanding of its functionality and how to effectively manage your diabetes with this system.

Tandem Control-IQ technology seamlessly integrates three key components:

- <span class="mark">a Dexcom or FreeStyle Libre sensor</span>

- <span class="mark">the Tandem t:slim X2 insulin pump,</span>

- <span class="mark">and the integrated Control-IQ technology.</span>

<span class="mark">The pump and sensor data can be viewed on th</span>e Tandem t:slim Mobile ap<span class="mark">p, and in selected regions like the US, you can even administer an insulin bolus via the app. Reports can be accessed via Glooko or Tandem Source, Tandem’s dedicated readout software. If you’re using a Dexcom sensor, the sensor data can be sent to the Dexcom Clarity and Share platform, allowing a family member or caregiver to monitor your sensor data remotely.</span>
