## **<u>1. Introduction</u>** 

Welcome to this module on the MiniMed 780G automated insulin delivery system. As one of the most widely used automated insulin delivery systems in many European countries, the MiniMed 780G offers a comprehensive solution for diabetes management.

This AID system seamlessly integrates three key components:

- the Guardian <span class="mark">4, Simplera Sync or Instinct sensor,</span>

- the MiniMed 780G insulin pump,

- an<span class="mark">d the integrated</span> SmartGuard algorithm.

The system allows you to monitor and manage your glucose levels effectively. By utilizing the CareLink platform, you can access detailed reports and analyze your data. Additionally, the MiniMed Mobile app enables convenient access to your glucose information on your mobile phone. For caregivers, the CareLink Connect app provides the ability to track the user's progress.

In previous general videos on automated insulin delivery systems, you learned valuable information, including how to compare automated insulin delivery systems using the CARES reference framework, how to choose the right system for your needs, how to interpret reports, and how to handle various scenarios such as hypo and hyperglycemia, high-fat meals, exercise, illness, swimming, and traveling.

In the upcoming videos, we will delve into the specifics of the MiniMed 780G, providing you with a step-by-step understanding of its features and functionalities. This structured educational approach will equip you with the necessary knowledge to navigate the system successfully.

It is important to note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your diabetes therapy.

The material has been reviewed by Dr. Henk-Jan Aanstoot,</span> a pediatrician-endocrinologist and founder of Diabeter in the Netherlands, and Sjoerd Aerts, a user of the MiniMed 780G system and administrator of the dutch FaceBook page about the MiniMed 780G. These individuals have contributed their expertise and insights to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the information provided in these videos.

By the end of this video series, you will have a comprehensive understanding of the MiniMed 780G system, including its performance according to the CARES paradigm, practical usage of the MiniMed 780G pump and MiniMed Mobile app, report generation and interpretation in CareLink, as well as effective strategies for managing special situations.

Whether you are a healthcare provider seeking to enhance your knowledge or an individual considering or already using the MiniMed 780G, these videos will provide valuable insights, tips, and tricks to facilitate optimal usage and improve your overall diabetes management experience.

We wish you the best of luck on your journey with the MiniMed 780G automated insulin delivery system!
