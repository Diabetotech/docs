## Transcript

Welcome to this video on the functionality of the twiist automated insulin delivery (AID) system.

The twiist system can operate with Loop automation on or off.

- When Loop is ON, the twiist AID system will automatically adjust your basal insulin delivery in response to your glucose as often as every 5 minutes, in an effort to bring your glucose into your Correction Range.
- When Loop is OFF, your basal will be delivered based on your scheduled basal rates.

## 4.1 Loop On

### Starting Loop

Once your sensor and pump are paired, you can turn on Loop via the settings.

If Loop is on, the twiist Status icon is a closed circle, the glucose prediction is projected on the charts and the basal insulin delivery is adjusted every 5 minutes.

### Using Workout Preset

The twiist app offers an exercise feature, called the Workout Preset, to prevent hypoglycemia during exercise. You can activate it via a heart-shaped icon on the bottom of the home screen. Once you tap it, you can choose to keep the workout preset active for 1 hour, 2 hours or until you choose to turn it off.

### Using Pre-Meal Preset

The twiist app also offers a more aggressive Pre-Meal Preset, to prevent hyperglycemia after meals. You can use the Pre-Meal Preset by tapping the second icon on the left on the bottom of the homescreen when Loop is on. The Pre-Meal Preset will stay active until you enter carbs.

### Bolus Delivery

When Loop is active, you can enter carbohydrates into the bolus calculator to receive an insulin dose recommendation. The default carb absorption time is set to 3 hours but can be adjusted to anywhere between 30 minutes and 23.5 hours.

To deliver a correction bolus without eating, tap the triangle icon on the status bar to see if a bolus is recommended.

### Suspending Insulin

You can temporarily stop insulin delivery through the app if your infusion set is disconnected or during special situations. To do this, tap the pump status icon on the home screen and tap suspend delivery.

## 4.2 Loop Off

When Loop is off, the twiist Loop icon will be an open circle, there will be no glucose prediction and insulin delivery is not adjusted every 5 minutes.

In Loop Off Mode the pump delivers your scheduled basal profile, or you can choose for a temporary basal. You can still enter carbs, bolus for meals, and give corrections with the integrated bolus calculator.
