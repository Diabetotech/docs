## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

Welcome to this comprehensive module on the Omnipod 5 automated insulin delivery system. Designed to provide you with a thorough understanding of the system, this module will guide you through its components, functionality, and practical application. By the end of this module, you will have gained valuable knowledge on how to effectively utilize the Omnipod 5 system in your diabetes management.

The Omnipod 5 automated insulin delivery system is widely utilized in the United States <span class="mark">and has recently made its way to Europe, with expectations of expanding to more countries in the near future. The system combines three key components:</span>

- <span class="mark">A glucose sensor like the Dexcom G6, Dexcom G7 or FreeStyle Libre 2 Plus, providing accurate and continuous glucose monitoring.</span>

- <span class="mark">The Omnipod 5 insulin pump, which shares similarities with the Omnipod DASH.</span>

- <span class="mark">And the integrated SmartAdjust algorithm, automatically adjusting insulin delivery based on glucose data.</span>

<span class="mark"></span>

<span class="mark">Reports from the system can be accessed and analyzed using the Glooko platform or the dedicated Omnipod Discover platform. Control of the pump can be managed through the Omnipod 5 Controller. In the US there is also an Omnipod 5 app available for iOS and Android phones. If you use a Dexcom sensor, the Dexcom G6 or G7 app plays a crucial role in the Omnipod 5 system, allowing family members to easily follow and monitor glucose levels through the Dexcom Follow app. When using a FreeStyle Libre sensor, glucose data can be shared with family members via LibreLinkUp, depending on regional availability.</span>

<span class="mark"></span>

Throughout this module, we will build upon the knowledge gained from our general videos on automated insulin delivery systems, covering topics such as comparing these AID systems using the CARES reference framework, selecting the right system for your needs, interpreting reports, and managing various scenarios such as hypo and hyperglycemia, exercise, illness, high-fat meals, alcohol, and travel.

It's crucial to remember that these videos are not intended as medical advice.It is recommended to consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

Their expertise and personal experience contribute to the comprehensive and reliable information provided in the videos.

By the end of this module, you will have gained a comprehensive understanding of the Omnipod 5 automated insulin delivery system, including its performance within the CARES paradigm, practical implementation using the Omnipod 5 Controller and app, report generation and interpretation using Glooko, and strategies for managing hypo and hyperglycemia, exercise, illness, high-fat meals, alcohol, and travel.
