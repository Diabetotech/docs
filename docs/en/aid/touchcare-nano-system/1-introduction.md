## **<u>1. Introduction</u>** 

Hi there, and welcome! In this Module, we’re going to introduce you to the Medtrum TouchCare Nano automated insulin delivery system. — a super convenient, tubeless insulin pump combined with a continuous glucose monitor. The TouchCare Nano System is designed to help people with diabetes manage their blood sugar easily, without the hassle of tubes or complicated set-ups.

The Medtrum TouchCare Nano system has three simple parts working together:

- The TouchCare Nano Patch Pump,

- The TouchCare Nano CG<span class="mark">M,</span>

- <span class="mark">And the APGO algorithm on your pump and transmitter that can be controlled via your phone or on your PDM.</span>

<span class="mark">The system allows you to manage your insulin delivery and monitor glucose levels with ease and flexibility. Through the EasyPatch app, EasyTouch app and EasyView web portal, you can access detailed reports, analyze trends, and adjust your diabetes management in collaboration with your healthcare tea</span>m. Family and caregivers can follow the glucose and insulin data through the EasyFollow app.

In previous general videos on automated insulin delivery systems, you learned valuable information, including how to compare automated insulin delivery systems using the CARES reference framework, how to choose the right system for your needs, how to interpret reports, and how to handle various scenarios such as hypo and hyperglycemia, high-fat meals, exercise, illness, alcohol, swimming, and traveling.

Now, we’re going to walk you through how the Medtrum TouchCare Nano system works, step by step. We’ll show you how to set it up, use it, and make the most of its features. Our goal is to make sure you feel confident and comfortable using this technology in your daily life.

It is important to note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your diabetes therapy.

The content of these videos has been developed by Dr. Inge van Boxelaer, founder of Diabetotech and an experienced endocrinologist at the St-Lucas Hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The material has bee<span class="mark">n reviewed by Dr. Sarah Siegelaar, an endocrinologist at Amsterdam UMC in the Netherlands, as well as Davor Skeledzija from Croatia, who’s daughter with type 1 diabetes has used the TouchCare Nano system. These individuals have contributed their expertise and insights to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the information provided in these vid</span>eos.

By the end of this video series, you will have a thorough understanding of the TouchCare Nano system, including its performance, daily practical usage, report interpretation, and strategies for managing specific scenarios.

Whether you are a healthcare professional seeking to expand your expertise or an individual considering or already using the Medtrum TouchCare Nano system, these videos will offer valuable knowledge, insights, and practical tips to help you optimize your diabetes management.
