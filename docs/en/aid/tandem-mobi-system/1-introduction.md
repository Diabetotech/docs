## **<u>1. Introduction</u>**

<span class="mark">Welcome to this module on Tandem Mobi System, the smallest durable automated insulin delivery system available in the U.S that can be fully controlled by a mobile device.</span>

In this comprehensive guide, we will delve into the intricacies of the Tandem Mobi System, providing you with a step-by-step understanding of its functionality and how to effectively manage your diabetes with this system.

Tandem Mobi System seamlessly integrates three key components:

- <span class="mark">the Dexcom G6 or G7 sensor,</span>

- <span class="mark">the Tandem Mobi insulin pump, controlled by the Tandem Mobi Mobile app on your iPhone</span>

- <span class="mark">and the Control-IQ+ technology that resides on the Tandem Mobi pump.</span>

<span class="mark">All sensor and pump data is sent to Tandem Source, Tandem’s dedicated data visualization platform. People can choose to use the dedicated sensor app, in which case data is also being sent to the Dexcom Clarity Cloud. This allows for family and caregivers to follow the glucose sensor data of their loved ones.</span>

<span class="mark">In our general module on automated insulin delivery systems, you have already gained valuable knowledge on comparing automated insulin delivery systems using the CARES reference framework, selecting the right system for your needs, interpreting reports, and managing specific situations with automated insulin delivery systems i</span>n general.

In the upcoming videos, we will provide detailed insights specifically tailored to the Tandem Mobi System, ensuring you receive a comprehensive and structured education on getting started. It's essential to remember that these videos do not provide medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your therapy.

<span class="mark">The videos were produced by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the AZ St-Lucas hospital in Ghent in Belgium. The content was reviewed by Gary Scheiner, a Certified Diabetes Care and Education Specialist from Integrated Diabetes Services in the US**.** Here you can see their disclosures. Their expertise and personal experiences ensure that the information presented in the videos is comprehensive and unbiased.</span>

By the end of this video series, you will have a comprehensive understanding of:

- How Tandem Mobi System performs within the CARES paradigm,

- Practical implementation on the Tandem Mobi insulin pump,

- Creating and interpreting reports using Tandem Source,

- And managing specific situations such as hypo and hyperglycemia, high-fat meals, exercise, illness, alcohol and travel. Lastly, some insights from a real user of the Tandem Mobi System.

Whether you are a caregiver seeking to better support individuals using Tandem Mobi System or a user of the system looking for a successful start, these videos will equip you with the knowledge, tips, and tricks to enhance your experience. Remember, your diabetes journey is unique, and Tandem Mobi System offers valuable tools to navigate it effectively.
