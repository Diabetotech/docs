## **<span class="mark"><u>4. How the Tandem Mobi Control-IQ technology works</u></span>**

<span class="mark">Welcome to this video, where we will discuss how the Tandem Mobi System works.</span>

<span class="mark">We will explain how to use the Tandem Mobi System in two modes: with Control-IQ technology on, also known as automode, and with Control-IQ technology off, also known as manual mode.</span>

### ***4.1 Tandem Mobi System with Control-IQ technology***

#### ***Starting Control-IQ technology***

<span class="mark">Access the Control-IQ technology feature from the Navigation bar, tap “Settings”, tap “Pump” then tap “Control-IQ”. To turn Control-IQ technology on, tap the toggle next to Control-IQ. Here, you need to input your weight and total daily dose of insulin. The adjustable values align with the indication of Control-IQ+ technology.</span>

#### ***Using Sleep & Exercise Activity*** 

Under the Actions menu, you can explore three additional settings: Activity, Sleep, and Sleep schedules.

\- Exercise: Activating this activity increases the treatment values to 140-160 mg/dl (or 7.8-8.9 mmol/l). A movement icon is displayed alongside the window on the home screen. Please note that Exercise Activity cannot be scheduled, and can only be activated or deactivated on the go.

\- Sleep: By enabling Sleep Activity, the treatment values are adjusted to 112.5-120 <span class="mark">mg/dl or 6.25-6.</span>7 mmol/l. A "zzz" icon appears next to the window on the home screen. You can manually toggle sleep mode on or off or set specific sleep schedules. Two schedules can be configured for fixed days of the week, such as weekdays and weekends, ensuring they don't overlap.

#### ***Adjusting Parameters***

In the Tandem Mobi System with Control-IQ technology, you have the ability to customize the basal rate, carb ratio, and correction factor settings. To access these options, navigate to the Settings on your Tandem Mobi App and select "Pump." From there, you can choose "Personal Profiles" to switch between different profiles or adjust the parameters within each profile within the "Time Settings". This allows you to fine-tune your insulin delivery according to your specific needs and preferences.

#### ***Giving an insulin bolus***

When delivering an insulin bolus, the process remains the same with or without Control-IQ technology enabled. It's generally recommended to follow the pump's bolus suggestions. At the end of a bolus, you can choose to convert it into an extended bolus. Pay close attention to the pump's bolus recommendations, as adjustments may be suggested based on your current glucose levels.

#### ***Suspending insulin***

To tempor<span class="mark">arily stop the pump, like during showers or swimming, you can access the “Actions” in the na</span>vigation bar and tap "Stop Insulin." <span class="mark">Restarting basal insulin delivery can also be done via "Actions".</span>

### ***4.2 Tandem Mobi System without Control-IQ technology (manual mode)***

If you want to use the Tandem Mobi pump without automated adjustment of the basal insulin delivery, you can toggle off the Control-IQ technology via the pump settings. The pump will deliver basal and bolus insulin according to your Personal profile settings. There is no suspend-before-low option available if you disable Control-IQ technology.

Throughout your Tandem Mobi system journey, it's important to maintain open communication with your healthcare provider for personalized guidance and support. They will be able to help you adjust settings and make necessary changes based on your individual needs. It may require some trial and error during your consultations to find the optimal settings for your insulin delivery.

By working together with your healthcare provider and staying resilient, you can effectively manage your diabetes and lead a fulfilling life. Stay empowered and prioritize your health and well-being.
