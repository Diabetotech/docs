## **<u>1. Introduction</u>** 

Welcome to this module about mylife Loop automated insulin delivery system. This relatively new automated insulin delivery system combination from YpsoMed uses the CamAPS FX algorithm that has been developed in the UK, and is the first automated insulin delivery system with the FreeStyle Libre 3 sensor.

The mylife Loop AID system consists of three main components:

- the Dexcom G6 or FreeStyle Libr<span class="mark">e 3 or 3 Plus se</span>nsor,

- the CamAPS FX algorithm as an app on your phone.

- and the mylife YpsoPump insulin pump.

The mylife CamAPS FX app is available for Android, with an iOS version coming soon. It allows generating and exporting reports as PDFs from the app. Additionally, users can connect the app to the Glooko platform for sharing data with healthcare professionals. Family and caregivers can track the user's progress through the Companion app, and SMS-based remote monitoring is also available.

In the previous general videos on automated insulin delivery systems, you learned about comparing automated insulin delivery systems, selecting the right system for you, interpreting reports, and managing specific scenarios such as hypo- and hyperglycemia, high-fat meals, exercise, illness, alcohol, and traveling.

In the upcoming videos, we will dive deeper into the mylife Loop AID system, exploring its features, functionality, and step-by-step operation. This comprehensive education will provide you with the necessary knowledge to confidently start and manage mylife Loop.

Please note that these videos are not intended as medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any adjustments to your diabetes therapy.

The videos were created by Dr. Inge Van Boxelaer, the founder of Diabetotech and an endocrinologist at the St-Lucas hospital in Ghent in Belgium. As a healthcare provider, you will be well-equipped to support individuals using mylife Loop, and as a user, you will have the knowledge and tools to ensure a successful start-up and make the most of mylife Loop. You will also gain valuable tips to simplify your experience with the system.
