## **<u>3. Linking components together</u>**

In this video, we will walk you through the process of linking the different components of mylife Loop and enabling data sharing capabilities.

Mylife Loop operates in the following way:

- Glycemia is measured every 1 to 5 minutes by either the Dexcom G6 or FreeStyle <span class="mark">Libre 3 or 3 Plus sensor.</span>

- <span class="mark">The Dexcom G6 transmitter or the FreeStyle Libre 3 transmitter sends the glycemia readings to the mylife CamAPS FX app on your mobile phone.</span>

- <span class="mark">The mylife CamAPS FX app calculates the insulin dose based on the received data and sends it to the mylife YpsoPump.</span>

- <span class="mark">The mylife YpsoPump delivers insulin boluses every 8 to 12 minutes, guided by the information from the mylife CamAPS FX app.</span>

- <span class="mark">Data from the mylife CamAPS FX app is sent to the CamAPS Cloud, which also stores insulin settings for future use in case of mobile phone switches.</span>

- <span class="mark">The mylife CamAPS FXapp can transmit data to Glooko for further analysis and reporting.</span>

- <span class="mark">Additionally, the mylife CamAPS FX app can be connected to the mylife CamAPS FX app in Companion mode, allowing caregivers to access real-time information.</span>

In this video, we will cover the step-by-step process of establishing the following connections:

- <span class="mark">Linking the Dexcom G6 or FreeStyle Libre 3 or 3 Plus sensor to the mylife CamAPS FX app.</span>

- <span class="mark">Connecting the mylife YpsoPump to the mylife CamAPS FX app.</span>

- <span class="mark">Establishing the link between the mylife CamAPS FX app and the CamAPS Cloud.</span>

- <span class="mark">Creating a Glooko account.</span>

- <span class="mark">Linking the mylife CamAPS FX app to Glooko Personal.</span>

- <span class="mark">Connecting the mylife CamAPS Fx app in Companion mode to the mylife CamAPS FX app.</span>

By successfully creating these links, you can ensure seamless integration of the mylife Loop system's components and enable effective sharing of data.

### ***3.1 Linking the sensor to the mylife CamAPS FX app***

When launching the mylife CamAPS FX app, you will have the option to select either the Dexcom G6 or the FreeStyle Lib<span class="mark">re 3 or 3 Plus sensor,</span> depending on your country. You can switch between the 2 sensors at any time. If you go to the sensor status screen via the menu, you can always choose which type of sensor you want to use.

- Follow these steps for the Dexcom G6 sensor:

  - If you haven't already done so, you can apply the Dexcom G6 sensor and transmitter on your skin.

  - If you are currently using the Dexcom G6 transmitter with the Dexcom G6 app, you need to disconnect it first: make a note of the serial number of the transmitter, delete the Dexcom G6 app, and remove the transmitter from your mobile phone's Bluetooth connections. It’s important not to stop the sensor in the app!

  - Enter the serial number of the Dexcom G6 transmitter in the mylife CamAPS FX app. The app will display a 30-minute counter, but the connection process usually takes only a few minutes.

  - Once the pairing is successful, you will see the "start sensor" option on the home screen. Tap on it and enter the 4-digit sensor code to initiate the 2-hour warm-up period for the sensor. If the sensor has already been started, the screen will show the sensor's glycemia, indicating that the installation is complete.

  - After 10 days, the sensor session will automatically stop, and the "start sensor" option will reappear on the home screen. If needed, you can manually stop the sensor earlier by selecting "stop sensor" from the hamburger menu at the bottom.

- The FreeS<span class="mark">tyle Libre 3 has no serial number for the transmitter or sensor code for the sensor. So start-up will be just a little smoother. Once you select the FreeStyle Libre 3 in the app, you can simply scan the sensor with your phone to start it. After starting, you’ll see a warm-up message with a 60-minute countdown. Once that’s complete, your sensor glucose reading will be displayed.</span>

### ***3.2 Linking the mylife YpsoPump to the mylife CamAPS FX app***

To link the mylife YpsoPump to the mylife CamAPS FX app, follow these steps:

1.  In the mylife CamAPS FX app, navigate to the settings section and select "insulin pump." Choose Ypsopump as the pump type.

2.  Ensure that Bluetooth is enabled on your mylife YpsoPump. Access the pump settings through the main menu and look for the Bluetooth icon. If there is a cross on the icon, tap on it to turn on Bluetooth.

3.  Tap on the serial number displayed in the app and select your country from the options provided.

4.  Put your mylife YpsoPump into Pairing Mode. This can be done by selecting the icon with the two Bluetooth logos in the pump settings.

5.  The pump will display a code that you need to enter into the mylife CamAPS FX app to establish the link. Enter the code as prompted.

If the pairing process is successful, you will see the serial number of your mylife YpsoPump, along with information about the insulin on board and the number of days since the insulin cartridge was replaced, in the menu.

Please note that when changing the infusion set, you will need to perform this action on the pump itself. To access the pump menu, you will temporarily have to disconnect from the mylife CamAPS FX app.

### ***3.3 Linking the mylife CamAPS FX app to the CamAPS cloud***

When you first open the mylife CamAPS FX app, you will be prompted to create an account that will link your information to the CamAPS cloud. It is important to remember your username and password for future reference, especially if you ever switch mobile phones to ensure you don't lose your settings.

In the event that your mobile device breaks, gets lost, or if you decide to switch to a different mobile, you can transfer the previously learned information to your new device. Simply reinstall the mylife CamAPS FX app on your new device and log in using your existing username and password. However, please note that if there are significant differences in your body weight and total daily insulin dose compared to what was previously used, the mylife CamAPS FX app will not download the learned information into the new app.

In such cases, the algorithm will restart its learning process from the beginning and require approximately two weeks to optimize itself again.

### ***3.4 Creating a Glooko account***

The pump and sensor data can be shared with your healthcare provider via Glooko.

We will describe how to create a Glooko account as a user via Glooko Personal, and as a healthcare professional via Glooko for Professional Use.

*\#1 Glooko Personal*

- <span class="mark">As a user, you can visit my.glooko.com and choose "Create Account."</span>

- <span class="mark">At a certain moment you will be able to enter a "proconnect code" that connects your account to your hospita</span>l.

- After completing the registration, you will receive an email with further instructions. It is also possible to receive this email directly from your healthcare provider.

- Create a password for your account and keep it accessible for future logins, especially when you need to upload data from your pump.

*\#2 Glooko for Healthcare Providers:*

1.  Healthcare providers or institutions can purchase a professional Glooko account directly from Glooko. You can then log in via my.glooko.com using the username and password provided by your hospital.

2.  Upon logging in, you will find an overview of the latest data uploads and a separate tab displaying an overview of your patients. On the top right corner you will find the "proconnect code" of your hospital.

3.  You can customize the patient list by sorting it according to your preferences or using flags to identify patients with high or low glycemic levels easily. You can add a new patient via the button in the top right corner. and you can add their email address to invite them to Glooko.

### ***3.5 Linking the mylife CamAPS FX app to Glooko Personal***

Within the mylife CamAPS FX app, you can access the Sharing feature from the menu. For User 1, you have the option to enter your email address associated with your Glooko Personal account as the username, along with the corresponding password for your Glooko account.

Please note that it may take up to 48 hours for your CamAPS data to be fully synchronized with Glooko.

By enabling the connection, the mylife CamAPS FX app will transmit its values to your Glooko Personal account at regular intervals of every 5 to 10 minutes. This allows for seamless transfer and monitoring of your data within the Glooko platform.

### ***3.6 <span class="mark">Linking the mylife CamAS FX app in Companion mode to the</span> mylife CamAPS FX app***

<span class="mark">Via the mylife CamAPS FX app in Companion mode , y</span>ou can allow up to 10 caregivers to follow your progress in real-time. Follow these two simple steps:

*Step 1*: From the menu of your mylife CamAPS FX app, navigate to the Share section. Enter the email address and username of the person you wish to grant access to. Once entered, click on the "Invite" button. This action will send an email to the designated person with detailed instructions.

*Step 2*: The recipient of the invitation will be prompted to install the mylife CamAPS FX app and create a new account on their CamAPS FX app.

Once they have done this, they can link their app to the companion, and select the person who has invited them. After a little while, the glucose and insulin data will start appearing. The CamAPS companion mirrors the information on the person's own CamAPS FX app. The glucose and insulin data as well as alerts will be shared in real time. The CamAPS companion can even have different alerts to the one set on the person's own CamAPS FX app if that is appropriate.

The functions that change insulin delivery such as the bolus calculator, Boost and Ease-Off are disabled for safety reasons. There may be times that the companion is not needed for example a teacher when the child is not at school. The companion can be made inactive in the child's CamAPS FX's share menu.

People can use both CamAPS companion and SMS alerts for remote monitoring purposes. Users will need a SIM card and a suitable Mobile plan to send sms's.
